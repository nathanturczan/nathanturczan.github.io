about

Nathan Turczan is a composer, developer, and educator creating non-linear audio experiences that render musical harmony as a tangible space for audiences to interact/play within. In 2021 he launched two music software tools: Scale Navigator Dashboard, a webapp for exploring musical scales in a network, and Scale Navigator MIDI VST, an "autotune for MIDI" plugin. Nathan is CTO of Chordas Collective (a vocal/electronic music ensemble), teaches Media + Web Development in the CalArts Music Technology department.
