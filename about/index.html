About

My projects start because I hit a musical idea that existing software can't express. Then I design and build the interface it needs.

Scale Navigator came from wanting to change harmony once and have every instrument follow. Ensemble Jammer came from wanting non-musicians to improvise together without wrong notes. SNaPS came from wanting samples to survive harmonic changes instead of restarting.

I design the interaction model, prototype, build, ship, and refine after people use it. Before this I was a product manager on creative software at SPG Studios and FutureCare. MFA in Music Technology from CalArts.
