With · Sia & David OReilly

Platforms · Web · Interactive characters

2022 Music Design

SOULS is a collection of 10,000 interactive characters by Sia and David OReilly, each one alive with its own ambient music. I designed the generative music system that gives every character a distinct sonic identity from one shared set of rules. The collection reached #3 on Billboard's music NFT chart in July 2022.
