Early Downhome Blues

Interactive exploration of Jeff Todd Titon's pitch transition network.

Walk through the melodic landscape of early blues music via an interactive pitch network from musicologist Jeff Todd Titon's seminal work Early Downhome Blues: A Musical and Cultural Analysis (Figure 64). Click notes on a musical staff to navigate valid note transitions, discovering how melodies move between pitches in early blues.

2024 Software
JavaScript / Tone.js / VexFlow / D3.js

Key Features:

Quarter-tone blue notes (E flat, E quarter-flat, E natural)
Karplus-Strong plucked string synthesis
Inflecting drone that bends to match microtonal melody
MIDI output with pitch bend for external instruments
D3 network graph visualization
Firebase-powered ensemble collaboration mode

Research Foundation:

Based on Jeff Todd Titon's groundbreaking musicological analysis of early blues recordings. The pitch transition network captures authentic blues characteristics including microtonal inflections, sink notes (C as resolution point), and hub notes (G connecting to multiple destinations).
