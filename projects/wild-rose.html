To A Wild Rose Growing Throughout the Multiverse

A procedurally generated album: 14 algorithmic remixes of Edward MacDowell's "To a Wild Rose".

Each bar transposes into a neighboring Pressing Scale via sample rate modulation. Modulation determined by randomly selecting from the current scale's maximally intersecting neighbors. Fully algorithmic. Python-scripted.
